# DeskHours Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-13

DeskHours is a browser extension that blocks websites on a schedule and provides a
focus timer.

## What we collect

Nothing. DeskHours does not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data.

## What stays on your device

All data you create — your blocklist, schedule, settings, focus/timer state, and your
master password verifier (a salted hash; the password itself is never stored) — is kept
in the browser's local extension storage on your device only. It is never sent to us or
to any third party.

## Network and tracking

DeskHours contains no analytics, no tracking, and no remote servers. It does not read
the content of your network requests; site blocking is performed with Chrome's
declarativeNetRequest API using local rules.

## Permissions

DeskHours requests broad host access (`<all_urls>`) only so it can block any site you
choose to add to your blocklist. It does not use this access to read, collect, or
transmit your browsing.
